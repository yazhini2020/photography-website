WHEN IS THE BEST TIME TO PHOTOGRAPH MY BABY?

The best time to photograph newborn babies is between 7 and 21 days. You will need to book in your session well in advance so give us a call as soon as you know your due date. We will make a provisional booking for 10-21 days after your due date, and we can amend it if your baby is early or late. Because newborn sessions can take up to four hours, we only book them Monday to Friday, and all newborn baby sessions start at 10 am. We only ever book in one newborn session a day, so there is no rushing your baby

DO YOU HAVE EXPERIENCE PHOTOGRAPHING NEWBORN BABIES SAFELY?

We have been photographing newborn babies for over nine years (over 700 babies and counting!) and have training in handling and posing babies safely. WILL FLASH PHOTOGRAPHY HARM MY BABY?

Absolutely not, I have never witnessed a baby react to the flash, asleep or awake. The light that is emitted from my studio equipment is no brighter than that which comes through your window at home on a bright overcast day.

DO I NEED TO BRING ANY PROPS OR ACCESSORIES TO MY NEWBORN SESSION?

As you will notice in our newborn gallery, we do not make use of props in our newborn baby sessions. We sincerely believe that beautiful photographs of your baby on their own or in your hands are more timeless and will be more valued by you and your child when they are older. However, if you have something special that you would like us to include in your pictures, please feel free to bring it along. We have a selection of outfits, fabrics and accessories that we can use to style your session. Once you have booked your session, we will send you a guide on what to bring to your session if you choose to have your shoot at a studio location.
